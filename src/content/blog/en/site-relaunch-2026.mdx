This site now runs on **Astro 6** with content collections for research projects, research ideas, and blog posts.

## What changed

- Bilingual routing uses `/` for English and `/zh/` for Chinese.
- New research entries are added as MDX files under `src/content/research/`.
- Comments on detail pages use Supabase auth (magic link or Google).

## For contributors

Add a new blog post by creating `src/content/blog/en/your-slug.mdx` (and optionally a Chinese counterpart under `zh/`).
